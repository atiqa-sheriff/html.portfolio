Atiqa's Movie Ranking

My Top 5 Movies of All Time:

Harry Potter Series:

Harry Potter (Daniel Radcliffe): The brave and determined young wizard who discovers his destiny to defeat the dark wizard Lord Voldemort.

Lord of the Rings Trilogy:

Frodo Baggins (Elijah Wood): The humble hobbit entrusted with the perilous task of destroying the One Ring and saving Middle-earth from darkness.

Titanic:

Jack Dawson (Leonardo DiCaprio): A passionate and free-spirited artist who falls in love with Rose aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

3 Idiots:

Rancho/Phunsukh Wangdu (Aamir Khan): An eccentric and brilliant student who challenges the traditional education system and inspires his friends.

Kuch Kuch Hota Hai:

Rahul Khanna (Shah Rukh Khan): A charming and popular college student who falls in love with Anjali but remains oblivious to her feelings.
